About

Philosophy: Empowering youth through education.

Mission: Our mission is to provide English education to out-of-school children which will enable them to explore many different national and global opportunities in the future.

Vision: We strive for a literate, and empowered Rwanda.

Goals: Expanding learning opportunities for all children in Rwanda and turning Isomo Program into a nationwide NGO which serves the bigger community.

History: After firsthand witnessing the impact growing up in poverty with no quality education, Emery Twajamahoro started Isomo Program in 2022 with just 23 children under a mango tree which since then has impacted nearly 500 students, thanks to government support and dedication.

Leadership: Our team includes dedicated high school graduates, volunteers, and community leaders under the leadership of founder Emery Twajamahoro.

Our Partners: We are supported by local government agencies, schools, and compassionate community members.
