About

Philosophy

Mission: To provide English education to out-of-school children.

Vision: A literate, empowered generation.

Goals: Expand learning opportunities for all children in Rwanda.

History

The Isomo Program started in 2022 with just 23 children and has grown to over 496 students thanks to community support and dedication.

Leadership

Our team includes dedicated educators, volunteers, and community leaders under the leadership of founder Emery Twajamahoro.

Our Partners

We are supported by local government agencies, schools, and compassionate community members.
