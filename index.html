Isomo Program

Empowering Out-of-School Children through English Education

About Us

The Isomo Program was founded by Emery Twajamahoro in 2022 with the mission of teaching English to out-of-school children in Rwanda. What started with just 23 learners has grown to 496 students, supported by the government and community.

Our Impact

  • 496+ students reached
  • 23 initial learners
  • 5 supporting schools
  • 1 big dream: Literacy for all
